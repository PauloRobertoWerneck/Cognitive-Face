This document describes the scope and the product overview. YCC is a yard and railway terminal management solution that allows the user to plan all yard switching activities, as well as coupling and releasing trains.
(Created on 10/2/2019.  Last modified 3/19/2020)

The purpose of this document is to specify system functionalities and/or the product to be developed, providing the development team the information required to meet the system requirements.
(Created on 10/2/2019.  Last modified 3/19/2020)  

3.2	Scope 

The scope of this document is to explain YCC functionalities, and to serve as a reference for the subsequent development process documents.
